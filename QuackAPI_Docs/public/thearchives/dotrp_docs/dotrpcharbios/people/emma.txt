Mrs. Emma de Groot, age 68. Emigrated with her husband Willem from the Netherlands after they got married. They have one daughter named Mina. 
After her husband passed away she took up the job of fisherman to make ends meet. She has taken up fishing because she loves the sea even though she hates the smell of fish.
Determined to enjoy her life on her own. The world is too beautiful not to.She still misses her husband and you may catch her singing one of the Dutch songs they used to sing together remembering the good times they shared.
